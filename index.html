Phases of UHack


Phase 1 : Idea Submission Online

Register and Submit your solution online before 2rd September, 2018 in uhack.in

Phase 2 : Idea Shortlisting

Teams will be shortlisted based on the idea submitted and a confirmation of the same will be sent via team@uhack.in

Phase 3 : Hackthon Phase

Be a part of the 30-hour National Level Hackathon on 26th and 27th of September, 2018 at SRM Institute of Science and Technology, City Campus, Vadapalani, Chennai
